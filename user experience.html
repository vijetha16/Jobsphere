User Experience (UX) Specialist

Company: JobSphere
Location: Remote
Salary: $70,000 - $90,000 per year

Job Description

We’re looking for a passionate and empathetic User Experience (UX) Specialist to join our team. You’ll work closely with cross-functional teams to create user-centered solutions that are intuitive, engaging, and accessible across all platforms.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct user research and usability testing to gather insights and validate design decisions.
  • Create personas, journey maps, and workflows to guide the user experience strategy.
  • Collaborate with designers, developers, and product teams to improve UX across platforms.
  • Advocate for accessibility, usability, and user-centered design principles.
  • Analyze user behavior through data, analytics, and feedback to drive iterative improvements.

Employment Status

  • Full-time

Workplace

  • Remote

Educational Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction, Psychology, Design, or a related field.
  • Equivalent work experience in UX or product design will also be considered.

Experience Requirements

  • 3+ years of experience in user experience design or research roles.
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating user-centered design and usability work.

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • Deep understanding of UX research methods and best practices.
  • Proficiency in tools like Figma, Adobe XD, Miro, or Axure.
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills.
  • Ability to turn research into actionable insights and design strategies.
  • Knowledge of usability standards and accessibility (WCAG).

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with A/B testing and behavioral analytics tools (Hotjar, GA, etc.).
  • Understanding of front-end development (HTML, CSS, JS basics).
  • Familiarity with agile or lean UX environments.
  • Background in psychology or cognitive science is a plus.

Benefits & Perks

  • Competitive salary with performance incentives.
  • Remote-first culture with flexible scheduling.
  • Professional development and training support.
  • Generous PTO, sick leave, and wellness programs.
  • Supportive and mission-driven team environment.
