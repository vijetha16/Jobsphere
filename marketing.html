Marketing Specialist

Company: JobSphere
Location: Remote
Salary: $60,000 - $80,000 per year

Job Description

We are seeking a talented and driven Marketing Specialist to join our dynamic remote team. You will be responsible for developing, implementing, and executing strategic marketing plans to attract potential customers and retain existing ones.

Key Responsibilities

  • Create and manage marketing campaigns across digital and traditional channels.
  • Collaborate with the design and content teams to produce engaging marketing materials.
  • Monitor and report on campaign performance using analytics tools.
  • Conduct market research to identify trends and opportunities.
  • Manage and grow the company’s social media presence.

Employment Status

  • Full-time

Workplace

  • Remote

Educational Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business Administration, or a related field.
  • Relevant work experience will be considered in place of a degree.

Experience Requirements

  • 2+ years of experience in a marketing role.
  • Proven track record of managing successful campaigns.

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • Strong knowledge of marketing tools and platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, HubSpot, Mailchimp).
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to analyze data and identify marketing trends.
  • Creativity, attention to detail, and organizational skills.
  • Team player with strong collaboration skills.

Preferred Skills

  • Experience in SEO and SEM strategies.
  • Graphic design or video editing skills.
  • Knowledge of paid advertising campaigns (Google Ads, Meta Ads).
  • Familiarity with CRM tools and marketing automation.

Benefits & Perks

  • Competitive salary with performance-based incentives.
  • Work from anywhere with flexible scheduling.
  • Annual professional development allowance.
  • Paid vacation, holidays, and personal days.
  • Supportive and creative work environment.
