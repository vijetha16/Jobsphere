IT Services Specialist

Company: TechSolutions Inc.
Location: Hybrid (Remote/On-site)
Salary: $75,000 - $100,000 per year

Job Description

We are looking for a skilled IT Services Specialist to join our technical support team. The ideal candidate will provide comprehensive IT support services, maintain system infrastructure, and ensure optimal performance of our technology environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide technical support and troubleshooting for hardware, software, and network issues.
  • Install, configure, and maintain computer systems and applications.
  • Monitor system performance and implement efficiency improvements.
  • Manage user accounts, permissions, and access rights.
  • Implement and maintain security protocols and data integrity measures.
  • Document IT processes, procedures, and service requests.

Employment Status

  • Full-time

Workplace

  • Hybrid (3 days remote, 2 days on-site)

Educational Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.
  • Relevant certifications (e.g., CompTIA A+, Network+, Microsoft Certified IT Professional).

Experience Requirements

  • 3+ years of experience in IT support or system administration.
  • Experience with cloud services (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud Platform).
  • Proven track record of maintaining and troubleshooting network infrastructure.

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • Proficiency in Windows and Linux server environments.
  • Strong knowledge of networking protocols and infrastructure.
  • Experience with virtualization technologies (VMware, Hyper-V).
  • Familiarity with cybersecurity principles and best practices.
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
  • Ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical users.

Preferred Skills

  • Scripting experience (PowerShell, Python, or Bash).
  • Knowledge of containerization technologies (Docker, Kubernetes).
  • Experience with IT service management tools (ServiceNow, Jira).
  • Understanding of DevOps practices and CI/CD pipelines.

Benefits & Perks

  • Competitive salary with annual performance bonuses.
  • Flexible hybrid work arrangement.
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
  • Professional development budget for certifications and training.
  • 401(k) matching program.
  • State-of-the-art equipment and tools.
